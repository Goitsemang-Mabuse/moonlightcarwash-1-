About Us

Our Story

Moonlight Car Wash is a local business based in Mabopane, providing reliable and affordable car cleaning services. The business started as a small walk-in car wash serving the local community. Over time, it has built a reputation for consistent service and customer satisfaction.

As customer needs evolve, Moonlight Car Wash aims to grow into a modern and premium service provider by introducing mobile car wash, valet services, and online bookings. We are proud to serve our community and look forward to continuing to grow alongside it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, convenient, and reliable car cleaning services while ensuring excellent customer satisfaction. We believe every vehicle deserves professional care, and every customer deserves a friendly and efficient experience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading premium car wash service in Mabopane and surrounding areas by offering innovative, eco-friendly, and customer-focused solutions.

Our Values

Quality: We use the best products and techniques for every wash.

Convenience: Our mobile service and online booking make car care easy.

Integrity: We treat every customer and vehicle with honesty and respect.

Community: We are proud to serve and support the people of Mabopane.
