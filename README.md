\## Project Overview



This project focuses on the design and development of a professional website for Moonlight Car Wash, a local car wash business based in Mabopane. The aim of the project is to transform the business from a basic walk-in service into a modern, high-end car care brand with a strong digital presence.



The website serves as a platform to showcase the business, its services, and its value to customers. It highlights not only traditional car wash services but also introduces premium offerings such as valet services and a mobile car wash, where services can be delivered directly to the customer’s location.



The development process includes planning, designing, and structuring the website using HTML, followed by styling and responsiveness using CSS. The project also focuses on improving user experience by ensuring easy navigation, clear service presentation, and accessible booking options.



Additionally, the website is designed to meet the needs of modern users by providing convenience, accessibility, and visually appealing content. It aims to increase customer engagement, improve brand image, and support business growth by making services more visible and easier to access.



Overall, this project demonstrates practical web development skills while addressing a real-world business need, bridging the gap between a local service and a professional online platform.



\---



\## Design and User Experience



The website will follow a sleek and premium design aesthetic that reflects the modern and professional nature of Moonlight Car Wash. The overall visual identity will focus on simplicity, elegance, and clarity to enhance user experience.



The colour palette will consist of deep charcoal (#212121) as the primary background colour to convey luxury and sophistication. This will be complemented by pure white (#ffffff) for text and content clarity, ensuring readability across all pages. Silver grey (#9e9e9e) will be used as a secondary accent colour for highlights, borders, and subtle design elements, while bold black (#000000) will provide contrast and depth throughout the design.



The typography system will combine modern and clean sans-serif fonts to maintain a professional appearance. The Montserrat font will be used for headings to convey strength and boldness, while Roboto will be used for body text to ensure high readability across different devices and screen sizes.



The design will follow a clear hierarchical structure, starting from H1 headings down to smaller text elements, ensuring consistency and easy navigation throughout the website.



The layout will be structured using a grid system to organise content effectively. High-quality car wash imagery will be used throughout the site to visually communicate the services offered. A sticky navigation bar will be implemented to allow users to easily access all pages at any time.



The homepage will feature a bold hero section with a strong call-to-action button, encouraging users to book services quickly and efficiently.

\## Part 2: Website Styling and Development



In Part 2 of the project, the focus shifted from basic structure (HTML) to the visual design and user experience of the website using CSS. The website was styled to achieve a modern, clean, and professional appearance that reflects the nature of the car wash business.



All main pages of the website were styled, including the Home page, About page, Services page, Bookings page, and Contact page. A consistent design was applied across all pages to ensure a unified look and feel. This included the use of a structured layout, consistent spacing, and a clear visual hierarchy.



The styling process also involved improving the responsiveness of the website to ensure that it displays correctly on different screen sizes such as laptops and mobile devices. Navigation was enhanced to improve usability, and a sticky/positioned header was implemented to allow easy access to different sections of the website.



In addition, several HTML errors identified in Part 1 were corrected, and parts of the website structure were refined to improve readability, maintainability, and overall performance. Background images, overlays, and modern layout techniques were used to enhance the visual appeal of the services section and other key areas of the website.



Overall, Part 2 focused on transforming the website from a basic structure into a fully styled, user-friendly, and visually appealing web application.
